Skalkaz Option Page

Language:
Basically, the extension only translates from English to any (but not English) language, particularly from English to your native language. The app visits the Google Translator site in the background to find and retrieve the required word. Once you set the desired language pair on the Google Translator site, the app will use that setup. So check out Google Translator and configure as you wish.

Usage:
  • let the extension run on local files (e.g. PDF) and/or in incognito mode by checking the proper checkboxes under Google Extensions.
  • right click on the selected word and choose the proper item. This is the only method, which works with PDF file.
  • select a word and click on the extension's icon (not prefered)
  • press a meta key = and single click on the word (Option saved. Refresh all the pages.)
  • double click on the word (recommended) (Option saved. Refresh on the pages needed.)
  • display a search box in the popup (Option saved.)
